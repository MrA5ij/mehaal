Eligibility Criteria

To maintain quality and brand integrity, partners must meet certain requirements:

  • Legal, registered business entity
  • Stable operational structure
  • Ability to provide first-line customer communication in their region
  • Ethical and compliant business practices
  • Capability to follow Mehaal.Tech AI's brand, technical, and integration guidelines
  • Execution capacity aligned with project size

All partners undergo verification and a formal onboarding review.

Partnership Requirements

Partners agree to:

  • Sign an NDA and partnership agreement
  • Comply with licensing frameworks
  • Protect confidential information
  • Follow brand guidelines and usage policies
  • Maintain service quality and reporting standards
  • Use only approved marketing materials
  • Ensure no misuse of AI systems

Failure to follow guidelines can result in suspension or termination of partnership rights.

Revenue & Engagement Model

Revenue structures vary by partnership type. Typical models include:

  • Commission-based returns
  • Discounted bulk licensing
  • Joint revenue-share on enterprise deals
  • Custom strategic models for large-scale operations

All financial terms are documented in the partnership contract.

Onboarding Process

  4. NDA + Agreement signing
  5. Technical orientation & brand alignment
  6. Activation of partner dashboard and resources
